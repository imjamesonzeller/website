Keep words concise

Shorter words (4–10 letters) generally produce more balanced grids. Mix lengths for added challenge.

Mind the delimiter

Using commas? Enter , as your delimiter. Need to split on new lines? Use \n.

The download button exports the puzzle as a CSV so you can tweak and print from your favorite editor.

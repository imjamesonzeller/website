Getting Started

  1. Download the latest universal `.dmg`, drag Tasklight.app into `/Applications`, and launch it once.
  2. Grant Accessibility access when prompted so the global hotkey can run anywhere.
  3. Open the settings window with ⌘ , to connect your Notion workspace via OAuth.
  4. (Optional) Enable Bring Your Own Key and store your OpenAI key securely for AI-powered parsing.
  5. Choose your database, configure the date property, and set the perfect shortcut. You’re ready to capture.

System Requirements

  • macOS 12 Monterey or newer (Apple Silicon & Intel supported)
  • Active Notion account with access to a target database
  • Accessibility permission for Tasklight to listen for the global hotkey
  • Internet connection for Notion OAuth and optional AI parsing
